## 📌 Project Overview

The **SuperStore Advanced Analytics Platform** is an enterprise-grade Business Intelligence solution built in **Power BI** that transforms raw transactional data into actionable business insights.

This project goes beyond traditional reporting by combining **data modeling, advanced DAX calculations, interactive analytics, and real-time What-If simulations** to support strategic decision-making.

The objective was not only to analyze historical performance but also to enable users to evaluate future business scenarios and understand the impact of operational decisions on profitability.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

* Monitor Sales, Profit, Quantity, and Customer Performance
* Identify top-performing and underperforming products
* Analyze regional and state-level performance
* Measure Year-over-Year growth trends
* Evaluate sales gaps and profit contribution
* Simulate business scenarios through dynamic What-If analysis
* Support data-driven strategic decisions

## 📊 Advanced DAX Development

The project includes a comprehensive set of advanced DAX measures built using industry best practices.

### Key Metrics

* Total Sales
* Total Profit
* Profit Margin %
* Sales Gap
* Profit Contribution %
* Year-over-Year Growth
* Best Product
* Worst Product
* Best State
* Worst State

### Advanced Logic

* Dynamic calculations using `SWITCH()`
* Context-sensitive measures using `SELECTEDVALUE()`
* Optimized calculations using `VAR`
* Custom conditional formatting
* Dynamic KPI calculations

### Custom Business Classifications

#### Discount Bands

* Low Discount
* Medium Discount
* High Discount

#### Quantity Bands

* Low Volume
* Medium Volume
* High Volume
